Introduction to Movies and Society

Movies are one of the most powerful forms of entertainment in today’s world. They influence thoughts, emotions, and behavior.

Cinema has become an important part of our daily lives for relaxation, learning, and inspiration.

  • Movies are a major source of entertainment
  • They reflect real-life situations
  • They influence emotions and thinking

Positive Impact of Movies

Movies inspire people and raise awareness about important issues like education and environment.

  1. Encourages creativity
  2. Spreads awareness
  3. Promotes culture
  4. Motivates people

Negative Impact of Movies

Some movies promote violence and unrealistic lifestyles, affecting viewers negatively.

  • Promotes violence
  • Creates unrealistic expectations
  • Affects young minds
  • Spreads stereotypes
